The system

Here's how
it works.

01
We show up.

Haymaker assigns a crew. We come to your practices and sessions with cameras. We capture the footage so you don't have to think about angles, lighting, or what to film. You just do what you already do.

02
We handle everything after.

The editing, the captions, the posting schedule, the platform strategy. While you're in the film room we're cutting clips. While you're in treatment we're scheduling posts. All of it runs without you.

03
You approve in the app.

Open the Haymaker app. Review what we made. One tap to approve, one tap to request changes. The whole thing takes under a minute. We schedule and post on your behalf.

04
Deals come to you.

When a brand wants to work with you, we bring the deal directly to the app — terms included, ready for your answer. No cold emails. No guesswork. Just offers you actually want.

You spend under an hour a week. Everything else is us.
