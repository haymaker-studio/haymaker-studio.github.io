The system

Here's how
it works.

01
We show up.

We come to your practices and sessions with cameras. We capture the footage so you don't have to think about angles, lighting, or what to film.

02
We handle everything after.

The editing, the captions, the posting schedule, the platform strategy. While you're in the film room we're cutting clips. While you're in treatment we're scheduling posts.

03
You approve in the app.

Open the Haymaker app. Review what we made. One tap to approve, one tap to request changes. The whole thing takes under a minute.

04
Deals come to you.

When a brand wants to work with you we bring the deal directly to the app — terms included, ready for your answer.
