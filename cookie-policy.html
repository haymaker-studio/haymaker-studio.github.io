Cookie Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 6, 2026

What this covers

This policy explains how Haymaker uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website (haymaker.com). The Haymaker iOS app does not use browser cookies — app-specific analytics are covered in our Privacy Policy.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember you and understand how you interact with it. Some cookies are essential for the site to work. Others are used for analytics or to improve your experience.

What we use

Strictly necessary cookies

These are required for the website to function. They cannot be turned off. They do things like keep you logged in to your Haymaker account on the web.

Purpose Example
Session authentication Keeps you logged in
Security CSRF protection tokens
Preference storage Saves your cookie consent choice

Analytics cookies

We use analytics tools — including Mixpanel, Amplitude, and Firebase — to understand how visitors use the Haymaker website. This helps us identify what's working and what isn't.

These cookies collect information like pages visited, time spent, and how you arrived at our site. The data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

Tool Purpose Data collected
Mixpanel User behavior analytics Pages viewed, events, session data
Amplitude Product analytics Feature usage, funnels
Firebase Performance monitoring Load times, errors

You can opt out of analytics cookies using the cookie banner when you first visit the site, or by contacting us at privacy@haymaker-studio.com.

No advertising cookies

Haymaker does not use advertising cookies. We do not run retargeting campaigns or share your browsing data with ad networks.

Your choices

When you first visit haymaker.com, you'll see a cookie banner with the option to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can update your preference at any time by clicking the link in the footer.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Note that blocking all cookies may affect how the site functions — in particular, you may not be able to stay logged in.

For analytics opt-outs directly with the providers:

  • Mixpanel: mixpanel.com/optout
  • Google / Firebase: myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy
  • Amplitude: amplitude.com/privacy

Changes

If we change the cookies we use or how we use them, we'll update this policy and the effective date above. Material changes will be communicated via a new consent prompt on the site.
