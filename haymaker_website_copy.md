## SECTION: HOW IT WORKS

**Eyebrow**
The system

**Headline**
Compete. Share clips. Approve.
Everything else is Haymaker.

**Step 1 — We show up**
Haymaker assigns a crew. We come to your practices, your competitions, your environment. We capture the footage so you don't have to think about angles, lighting, or what to film. You just do what you already do.

**Step 2 — We build the content**
Every clip is scripted, edited, captioned, and formatted for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. We handle the hooks, the pacing, the trend awareness. You get a finished post ready for your approval.

**Step 3 — You approve in the app**
Open the Haymaker app. Review what we made. One tap to approve, one tap to request changes. The whole thing takes under a minute. We schedule and post on your behalf.

**Step 4 — Deals come to you**
As your audience grows, brand opportunities follow. Haymaker identifies the right partnerships, negotiates the terms, and brings deals to you in the app ready to accept or decline. No cold emails. No guesswork. Offers you actually want.

**Step 5 — You see everything**
Your follower count, views, engagement, and NIL earnings — tracked in real time in the Haymaker app. You always know where you stand and what's coming next.

**Closing line**
Your sport is already worth watching. We make sure the right people are watching it.

## WHAT WE BELIEVE

**Eyebrow**
Our philosophy

**Headline**
Four things we believe
that shape everything we build.

---

**1. NIL should be a system, not a series of exceptions.**

Most programs handle NIL case by case — one athlete, one deal, one moment at a time. There's no infrastructure behind it. No repeatability. No compounding value.

Haymaker is built differently. Every athlete who joins gets the same professional workflows, the same content systems, the same brand development process. It scales because it's designed to. The goal isn't to help a handful of athletes. It's to build something that works for every athlete in the program.

---

**2. Every sport deserves real infrastructure.**

Whether you have 500 followers or 50,000, Haymaker meets you where you are.

Athletes with existing audiences get professional systems to amplify and scale what's already working. Athletes starting from zero get real infrastructure to build their first meaningful presence. The tools don't change based on your sport or your starting point. Everyone gets the full system.

NIL should be inclusive. Not reserved for the athletes already in the room.

---

**3. The goal isn't the deal. It's what you build.**

One-off brand deals are fine. A personal brand that generates opportunities for the next ten years is better.

Haymaker isn't in the business of placing athletes in campaigns and moving on. We're in the business of helping athletes build audiences, develop their voice, and create something durable — content, reputation, reach — that belongs to them long after the last competition. The NIL revenue is a byproduct of building something real. We focus on the something real.

---

**4. Your sport is already worth watching.**

You don't need a personality transplant or a content strategy degree. The training footage, the competition moments, the access you already have as a college athlete — that's the raw material most creators would do anything to have.

Haymaker builds the system around it. You keep doing what you do. We make sure people see it.

# COPY NOTES & DECISIONS

**Tone**
Confident, direct, progression-oriented. Never hype. Never hustle culture. The brand believes in the athlete — and speaks to them like a serious operator would.

**Voice**
First person plural (we/our) for Haymaker throughout. Never passive. Never generic.

**What we never say**
- "Unlock your potential"
- "Take your brand to the next level"
- "Empower" (any usage)
- "Game-changer"
- "All-in-one platform"

**The core tension we always resolve**
Athletes want to build something real. They don't have time. Haymaker removes the time problem without removing the athlete from the process. They stay in control. We do the work.
